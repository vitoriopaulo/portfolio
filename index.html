About



I am a Brazilian-American Architect & Designer. I've experienced a wide range of design genres in my trajectory, such as: Architecture, Furniture, Interiors, Communication, and Webdesign as well. Nowadays I devote efforts to improve skills in web-development, both front and back-end development, as well as the matters related to UX|UI design. I am based in Boston, MA. USA since 2010, and currently I am enrolled in the WDI class20 in General Assembly.

It's being a life changing experience: I've been developing my tech skills fast and learning all the state-of-the-art technologies of the industry: HTML5, CSS, SCSS, JAVASCRIPT, jQuery, Ajax, Ruby, Ruby on rails, SQL, PostegreSQL, GIT, GITHUB, HEROKU and so much more. At this point of my life I've already accomplished my goals as an Architect and Designer and I am looking straight ahead for a career shift into the tech industry.

Based in Boston but with my eyes in the world, my goal as a Full-stack Web-developer is to design products that are functional, aesthetically pleasant and that establish a strong emotional connection with the web user. That tells all about my vision, my mission, my passion, my dreams.
