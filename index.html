About



I am a Brazilian-American Architect & Designer. I've experienced a wide range of design genres in my trajectory, such as: Architecture, Furniture, Interiors, Communication, and Webdesign as well. Nowadays I devote efforts to improve skills in web-development, both front and back-end development, as well as the matters related to UX|UI design, Information Architecture and usability. I am based in Boston, MA. USA since 2010, and just turned a General Assembly Boston alumn from the WDI (Web Development Immersive) class 020.

It was a life changing experience: I've developed tech skills fast and learned state-of-the-art technologies of the industry: HTML5, CSS, SCSS, JAVASCRIPT, jQuery, Ajax, Ruby, Ruby on rails, SQL, PostegreSQL, GIT, GITHUB, HEROKU and more mentored by a talented team of web dev practictioners. At this point of my life I've already accomplished my goals as an Architect and Designer and I am looking straight ahead for a career shift into the tech industry.

Based in Boston but with my eyes in the world, my goal as a Full-stack Web-developer is to design products that are functional, aesthetically pleasant and that establish a strong emotional connection with the web user. That tells all about my vision, my mission, my passion, my dreams.


Portfolio

This is my Capstone project from WDI 020 Fall Class Boston. The app is a SPA (single page application), with basic authentication requirements (Sign Up, Sign In, Change Password and Sign Out). The application is designed as a database management system for movies, where the registered user can make CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) interactions with the DB. The front-end was built with Html5, Css3, Javascript, jQuery, Ajax. The back-end was developed on top of the technologies Ruby, Rails and Heroku.
